# Instructions for Setting Up Company Email and Social Media Accounts

## Company Email
- Suggested Email Format: 
  - aiagent@owlbanapparel.com
  - support@owlbanapparel.com
- You can create the email through your domain provider or a service like Google Workspace or Microsoft 365.

## Social Media Accounts
1. **Facebook**
   - Go to [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com).
   - Click on "Create New Account."
   - Fill in the required information and choose a username related to your AI Agent.

2. **Twitter**
   - Go to [Twitter](https://www.twitter.com).
   - Click on "Sign up."
   - Follow the prompts to create an account, using a handle that reflects your AI Agent.

3. **Instagram**
   - Go to [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com).
   - Click on "Sign up."
   - Use an email address and create a username that represents your AI Agent.

4. **LinkedIn**
   - Go to [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com).
   - Click on "Join now."
   - Fill in the details to create a company page for your AI Agent.

Feel free to reach out if you need further assistance with any of these steps!
