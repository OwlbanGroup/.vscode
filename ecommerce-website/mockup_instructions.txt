# Instructions for Creating Apparel Mockups Using Canva

## Step 1: Access Canva
- Go to [Canva](https://www.canva.com).

## Step 2: Create a New Design
- Click on "Create a design" and select "Custom dimensions."
- Set dimensions suitable for apparel mockups (e.g., 2000 x 2000 pixels).

## Step 3: Upload the Lion Logo
- Click on "Uploads" in the left sidebar.
- Upload the lion logo you downloaded from the website.

## Step 4: Add Apparel Templates
- Search for "hoodie mockup," "hat mockup," or "sweatshirt mockup" in the templates section.
- Choose a template that fits your design needs.

## Step 5: Position the Logo
- Drag the lion logo onto the apparel template.
- Adjust the size and position as needed.

## Step 6: Download Your Mockup
- Once satisfied with the design, click on the "Download" button in the top right corner.
- Choose PNG or JPEG format for high-quality images.

## Step 7: Repeat for Other Products
- Repeat the process for hats and sweatshirts using their respective templates.

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or need further assistance!
